In his appeal before Surat court, Rahul Gandhi says was mistreated, sentenced to attract disqualification as MP

Rahul Gandhi's appeal argues that the disqualification of an elected representative "essentially interferes with the choice of the electorate in a free and fair election".

Surat |

Updated; April 4,2023 10:43 IST

Congress leader Rahul Gandhi with his sister and party's General Secretary Priyanka Gandhi arrives at Delhi airport, after filing an appeal in Surat District Court challenging his conviction in a defamation case, in New Delhi, Monday, April 3, 2023. (PTI Photo)
